UI/UX - mobile | Study Buddy Concept

Study Buddy is an application concept that I came up with to help students make friends/study buddies easier. Those that have trouble or are too shy talking to their classmates in real life can use the application to help ease into it. From there they can hopefully build a lasting friendship or connection for the future.

Pay as you go is a mobile application that helps ease up the process of filling up gas. Users can choose from getting the type of gas they want, how much of it they want, and either wanting it delivered to their doorstep or pre-paying before going to fill it up themselves.
